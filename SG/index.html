    Our Products

    Protein

    Gold Premium 100% Whey Protein by Sport Generation is a high quality whey isolate and concentrate protein blend containing 23 grams of protein per serving (30 g). High-quality Italian raw materials provide a good level of nitrogen and essential amino acids that the body needs to ensure the growth and maintenance of muscle tissue. Thus, the proteins present in Gold Premium 100% Whey Protein contribute to the development and maintenance of muscle mass, as well as supplement your daily diet with protein.

    BCAA

    This supplement contains 2500 mg of L-Leucine, 1250 mg of L-Isoleucine and 1250 mg of L-Valine. BCAAs are widely recognized in the bodybuilding world as helping to build and repair muscle. These amino acids are considered essential because they cannot be synthesized by the body, but are nevertheless necessary for the proper functioning of the body and muscles. Therefore, BCAAs should be consumed daily with food. In the context of intense and regular sports activities, which significantly increases the need, it is advisable to supplement the intake of amino acids with nutritional supplements.
